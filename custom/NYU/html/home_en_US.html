NYU Returns & Collections Access
  • Physical library materials can be requested for in-person pick-up, delivery, or digital scan.
  • Request options (“Request / Request Scan”) are found on the details page of a particular item.

New Books & More Features
  • Students and instructors can search for collections that are digital and multi-use using the Always Available Online filter.
  • Instructors can add “Always Available Online” items to Course Reserves in one-click via Add to Course Reserves (Instructors Only) link.

Need Help?
  • Use Ask A Librarian or the “Chat with Us” icon at the bottom right corner for help starting a search or understanding collections available.

Additional Resources

  • Use Interlibrary Loan for materials unavailable at NYU
  • Discover subject specific resources using expert curated research guides
  • Explore the complete list of library services
  • Reach out to the Libraries on our social media
  • Search WorldCat for items in nearby libraries

Using Bobcat (Library Catalog)

What is in Books & More?

Using the search bar on this page, you can find:

  • books / e-books
  • journals / e-journals
  • videos and sound recordings
  • offsite materials
  • special collections

Tools to help with your search:

  • browse journals by title
  • find an article by citation

Resources are across all of NYU’s New York and global libraries.

Looking for Articles or Databases?

Use the Articles & Databases tab to find:

  • articles from multidisciplinary databases
  • databases by title or by subject area

Note: For these resources, you must use the Articles & Databases tab, and NOT the search bar on this screen.
