How Do I get This Item?

  • Self Service: If it's listed as "Available" from the "Main Collection" (not "Main Collection Offsite") find it on the shelf whenever stacks are open. Check it out at the Circulation Desk. Use our call numbers and Bobst floor maps resource for help.
  • Locker Pickup: Allow several business days for processing. You’ll have 5 days to pick up your item once it’s is ready. Contactless collection whenever the building is open.
  • Bobst Circulation Desk Pickup: Allow several business days for processing. You’ll have 10 days to collect your item once it’s ready. In-person pickup during service hours.
  • Scan Requests: Limited to 1-2 chapters of a book. Generally ready in several business days.
  • If an Item is Not Available at NYU Libraries: Eligible borrowers can request the item through Interlibrary Loan (ILL) or EZBorrow.
