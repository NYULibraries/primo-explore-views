Announcements & Updates

New Books & More Features Summer 2020

"Always Available Online" filter tool

Helps you find ebook, video, and music resources ideal for your course.

  • Searches more than 2.8 million multi-user ebooks, videos, and music from over 100 publishers and databases.
  • Multi-user resources allow all your students to use the resource at the same time!

Instructors: Add “Always Available Online” resources to your course with one click from the catalog.

  • Eligible items now have an “Add to Course Reserves (Instructors Only)” link.
  • Library staff will add a stable link to the item to your course.
