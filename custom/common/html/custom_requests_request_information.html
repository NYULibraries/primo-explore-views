Explanation of Request Options

Users may see one or more of the request options below:

  • Request:
    • Locker pick-up at Bobst and Dibner Library
    • Home delivery for Faculty and PhD students in the continental U.S. 48 only
    • Pick-up at IFA or ISAW Libraries
  • Request Scan: Electronically delivered scan of no more than 1-2 chapters of a text.
    • To get a photocopy of the Table of Contents, submit a scan request with the phrase “TOC” in both the “Title” and “Pages” fields.
    • Limit to 5 scan requests at a time to avoid delays or cancellations

Note that specific NYU Libraries (e.g., AFC, IFA) may have their own requesting policies. Visit specific library web pages (opens in a new window) for more information.

If the item is not available at one of our libraries, eligible borrowers will be redirected to request through Inter-Library Loan (ILL)(opens in a new window) .

To see and manage current requests, visit My Requests Page(opens in a new window) . For general information on eligibility and request options, visit the Library's Collection Access page (opens in a new window) .
