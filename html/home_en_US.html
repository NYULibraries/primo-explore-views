Welcome to the Catalog of the Patricia D. Klingenstein Library of the New-York Historical Society

The majority of the Library's collections have been processed and cataloged, and may be found by searching the online catalog. If you are having trouble with your research, please contact the Library's reference staff

All researchers visiting the Patricia D. Klingenstein Library must register electronically, either remotely or on-site, before beginning their research. In addition, a current, government-issued photo ID must be presented upon arrival at the library. To begin the registration process, click on "Collections Request System" in the "My Workspace" box on the right.

Additional links at right will take you to finding aids for the manuscript and graphic material collections, the Library's digital collections, inventories of the Library's printed maps and hotel files, and a list of electronic resources available on-site only at the Patricia D. Klingenstein Library.

IMPORTANT. A small portion of the Library’s collections are now stored offsite. Any item with the designation of “Offsite” on a catalog record may be requested for onsite use in the Library’s reading room. Please keep in mind that it will take between two (2) and five (5) business days for collections to arrive, and you should plan your research accordingly. A list of call number ranges being stored offsite can be found here.
